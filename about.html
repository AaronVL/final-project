Ben Dover

CEO of Whole Lotta Dragons

Ben Dover has always had an interest in dragons. Ever since he was a little kid, he would spend all of his time watching movies about dragons, drawing dragons, he even dressed as a dragon for Halloween for seventeen years straight. As an adult, he decided he wanted to share his love of dragons with the world and started a company called Whole Lotta Dragons with an aim to:

  • Teach others about dragons
  • Showcase his art
  • Perhaps one day meet a dragon who happens upon his website
Please spread the word around about Whole Lotta Dragons.
